Gameplay Instructions

The goal? Find all of the memes! Scattered throughout the map are a total of six memes. You can track your meme progress using the progress bar at the top of the screen.
Once you have successfully found all of the memes, head to The House at the end of the stage to win! Also, be sure to avoid enemies and the Jankerwave.
Some memes may have some interesting effects. And, if you mess up that badly, reloading the page restarts the game. Also, be prepared, for you may encounter some meme-based easter eggs along the way!
Use the arrow keys and space bar to move. Good luck!
